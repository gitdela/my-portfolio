About Me

Hi, I'm Ken Nartey, a self-taught front-end developer with a passion for creating highly functional yet minimalist web pages. My Services

Web Design

I specialize in using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create responsive designs that function across different devices and browsers. I focus on delivering an exceptional user experience by paying attention to details, such as typography, color schemes, and navigation. My design service includes wireframing, prototyping, and development, as well as ongoing maintenance and support. I pride myself on delivering high-quality work while maintaining clear communication with my clients throughout the design process.

UI/UX Design

My design process starts with research, where I gain an understanding of the target users, their needs, and the problem that the design aims to solve. I then create prototypes, which I test with users to ensure that the design is user-friendly and functional. Once the design is finalized, I use tools such as Figma and Adobe XD to create high-fidelity designs that are ready for development. My design service includes a focus on user experience, with attention to details such as typography, color schemes, and layout. I pride myself on delivering high-quality work that meets the needs of my clients and their users.
